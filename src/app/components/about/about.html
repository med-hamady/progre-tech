About Proger Technology

Your trusted partner in digital transformation and innovation

Proger Technology is a leading Mauritanian software and networking company dedicated to delivering innovative digital solutions that empower businesses to thrive in the modern digital landscape.

Founded on the principles of excellence, innovation, and reliability, we combine deep technical expertise with a commitment to understanding our clients' unique challenges. To empower businesses across Africa and beyond with cutting-edge technology solutions that drive growth, innovation, and digital transformation, while maintaining the highest standards of quality and client satisfaction.

Our Vision

To become the leading technology partner in the region, recognized for our technical excellence, innovative solutions, and unwavering commitment to helping businesses succeed in an increasingly digital world.
